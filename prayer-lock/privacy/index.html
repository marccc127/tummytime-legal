Privacy Policy

Prayer Lock · Effective 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prayer Lock (the “App”), operated by Sanctuary Digital (Marc Philipp Hansen), handles your information. The App is designed to keep your prayer habit and app-blocking choices private and on your device.

In short: Prayer Lock has no account system and no backend of its own. Your name, prayer progress, streak, sanctuary progress, and selected apps stay on your device.

1. Who we are

Provider: Sanctuary Digital (Marc Philipp Hansen). Contact: info@familyjourney.app.

2. Information stored on your device

The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • Your first name, used to personalize on-screen text.
  • Your onboarding answers, such as prayer rhythm and goals.
  • Your prayer history, streak, stones, and sanctuary progress.
  • Your settings, such as reminder time, motion, and haptic preferences.
  • Your Screen Time / Family Controls app selection tokens. These are opaque Apple-provided tokens used by iOS to shield the apps you choose.

Deleting the App removes local App data from your device. iOS may separately manage Screen Time permissions in system settings.

3. App locking and Screen Time permissions

Prayer Lock uses Apple’s Family Controls, Device Activity, and Managed Settings APIs to let you choose apps that should stay locked until prayer is complete. Apple’s system permission sheet and app picker are provided by iOS. We do not receive the names of your selected apps on our servers, and we do not operate a server that could receive them.

4. Third-party services

The App may connect to Supabase to sync Prayer Circle data such as your profile name, circle membership, invite status, admin role, and daily prayer/excuse status. Supabase data is protected by Row Level Security so circle data is only available to authorized circle members and admins.

5. No selling, no advertising

We do not sell your data. The App contains no third-party advertising.

6. Spiritual content

Prayer prompts and scripture-based content are intended for personal spiritual reflection. They are not professional, medical, psychological, financial, or legal advice.

7. Data retention

On-device data remains until you delete it, reset it, or uninstall the App. Prayer Circle data may remain in Supabase until you leave a circle, an admin removes you, or you request deletion.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, including under EU/UK GDPR, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Most App data is under your direct control on your device. For Prayer Circle data requests, contact us at info@familyjourney.app.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
